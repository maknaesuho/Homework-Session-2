Hello, I am from Latvia

This is a small country in the Baltic States
However, it is really beautiful and has a lot to uncover!
Here are my top 3 recommendations:

Riga, the capital city of Latvia, is a charming blend of medieval and Art Nouveau architecture. Explore its historic Old Town, visit the iconic Riga Central Market, and indulge in the vibrant nightlife along the cobblestone streets.
Located on the shores of the Baltic Sea, Jurmala is a picturesque coastal resort known for its sandy beaches and charming wooden architecture. Relax by the sea, take a leisurely stroll along the famous Jomas Street, and experience the rejuvenating spa culture that the town offers.
Nestled in the Gauja National Park, Sigulda is a nature lover's paradise with its stunning landscapes, medieval castles, and outdoor adventure opportunities. Visit the iconic Turaida Castle, explore the mysterious Gutmanis Cave, and enjoy activities such as hiking, bobsledding, and cable car rides amidst breathtaking surroundings.
